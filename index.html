FreeCaptcha

Simple and Effective Bot Protection for your Forms

Why FreeCaptcha?

FreeCaptcha is a lightweight JavaScript library designed to protect your web forms from automated bots. It's easy to integrate and provides a simple yet effective honeypot technique to reduce spam submissions.

Features

  • Easy CDN integration
  • Invisible honeypot field
  • Lightweight and fast
  • Customizable options

Get Started

Include FreeCaptcha in your HTML:

<script src="free-captcha.js"></script>

And FreeCaptcha will automatically protect all forms on your page.

Basic Usage

To use FreeCaptcha, simply include the script in your HTML file before the closing <body> tag. FreeCaptcha will automatically attach to all forms on the page and add the honeypot protection. Try submitting this demo form:
